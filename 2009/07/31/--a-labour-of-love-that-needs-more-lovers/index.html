--A labour of love that needs more lovers

Posted on 2009-07-31

Man of action. Man of ambition:

[Tango Vía](http://www.tangovia.org/), the organization this tango double-bass player from the famous [Orquesta El Arranque](http://www.orquestaelarranque.com.ar) presides, is up to nothing less than archiving every single tango recording in a super-high definition, archive-grade digital format, following strict technical processing that will drastically enhance the sound quality to make it an almost-perfect match to the pristine original. > > [Buenos Aires Herald](http://buenosairesherald.com/PrintedEdition/View/4262) > >
